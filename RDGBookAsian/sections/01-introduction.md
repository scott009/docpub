# Introduction

## Welcome to Recovery Dharma

This book presents a path to recovery from addiction based on Buddhist principles and practices. The teachings contained here have been adapted specifically for those struggling with addiction and seeking a spiritual path to freedom.

## How to Use This Book

This book is organized into three main parts:
1. **The Four Truths of Recovery** - Understanding the nature of addiction and recovery
2. **The Eightfold Path** - Practical guidelines for living in recovery
3. **Developing a Practice** - Tools and techniques for daily practice

## The Recovery Dharma Approach

Recovery Dharma offers a peer-led, Buddhist-inspired approach to recovery from addiction. The program is:
- Non-theistic and inclusive
- Based on Buddhist philosophy and meditation practices
- Focused on community support and personal empowerment
- Free and open to all
