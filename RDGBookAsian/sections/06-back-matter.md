## About Recovery Dharma

Recovery Dharma is a peer-led movement and community of support for anyone seeking freedom from the suffering of addiction. Our approach is based on Buddhist principles and practices, adapted specifically for recovery.

### Our Values
- **Peer support** - We help each other
- **Inclusivity** - All are welcome
- **Accessibility** - Free and open to all
- **Empowerment** - Taking responsibility for our recovery
- **Community** - We recover together

### Our Vision
A world where anyone seeking recovery from addiction has access to Buddhist-inspired, peer-led support.
